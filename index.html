Article Reader

Paste an article URL. The page extracts readable text and reads it aloud using your phone or browser voice.

Premium voices (Cloudflare)
Stored only in this browser. Article text is sent to Cloudflare for synthesis.
GitHub Pages cannot run a private backend, so this version uses the browser's built-in speech synthesis instead of OpenAI TTS. Article extraction uses Jina Reader by prefixing the URL with https://r.jina.ai/; some sites may block extraction or hit rate limits.
